Features

Feeling bored during quarantine? Looking for the adventure of a lifetime? Don't worry any longer, Stardew Valley Education Edition has you covered!

Create the farm of your dreams

Turn your overgrown fields into a lively and bountiful farm!

Learn to live off the land

Raise animals, go fishing, tend to crops, craft items, or do it all! The choice is yours...

Become a part of the local community

Pelican Town is home to over 30 residents you can befriend!

Explore vast, mysterious caves

Encounter dangerous monsters & valuable treasures deep underground!

How to Play

  1. Choose a place to inherit: near the sea, in the open land, or deep in a dense forest
  2. Grow plants, mine ores, and gather materials to improve your farming equipment!
  3. Run around in the bustling town of Stardew Valley and explore what it has to offer!
